Who We Are

The USC Omicron Chapter of Gamma Iota Sigma, also known as Risk Management Society at USC, is a student organization for prospective, aspiring, and current risk management and insurance professionals. We hold events throughout the year to rigorously prepare students for a variety of career tracks, including underwriting, risk management, consulting, and actuarial science. Our vast array of membership benefits will ensure that you find success in recruitment, internships, and academics.

Our mission

To help sustain, promote, and foster student interest in the insurance, risk management, and actuarial professions through engagement, mentorship, and learning opportunities with industry professionals.
